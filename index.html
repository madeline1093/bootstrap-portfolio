About me

A short explanation of who I am.

My name is Madeline Story, and I am currently an Employee Experience Specialist at Strive Consulting. I am basically the snack and catering guru for my company, and lead the planning of all major events including our Employee Summer Apprecation Event and Holiday Party. I have the opportunity to work closely with a number of consultants in a variety of technical fields. This exposure led me to gain connections at a number of different coding bootcamps. In late 2017 I decided that the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp would best fit my career goals, and I dove headfirst into the program in February of 2018.

At the end of this program, I will have the skills equivelant to that of a Junior Front End Developer. I aim to change my career path with these skills and further my growth as a web developer.
